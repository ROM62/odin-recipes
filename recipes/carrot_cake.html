Carrot Cake

Description

A moist and flavorful carrot cake with cream cheese frosting.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups of grated carrots
  • 1 cup of brown sugar
  • 1 cup of raisins
  • 4 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups of white sugar
  • 1 cup of vegetables oil
  • 2 teaspoons of vanilla extract
  • 1 cup of crushed pineapple, drained
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 4 teaspoons ground cinnamon
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons baking soda
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 cup chopped walnuts

Steps

  1. Step 1: Gather all ingrediens
  2. Step 2: Combine grated carrots and brown sugar ini a medium bowl. Let sit for 1 hour, then stir in raisins.
  3. Step 3: Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour two 10-inch round cake pans. Beat eggs in a large bowl until light.
  4. Step 4: Gradually beat in white sugar, oil, and vanilla. Stir in pineapple.
  5. Step 5: Combine flour, cinnamon, baking soda, and salt in a separate bowl, then stir into egg mixture until absorbed.
  6. Step 6: Stir in carrot mixture and walnuts. Pour evenely into the prepared pans.
  7. Step 7: Bake in the preheated oven until an inserted toothpick comes out clean, 45 to 50 minutes.
  8. Step 8: Cool for 10 minutes before removing cake layers from the pans; let cool completely.
